  Regulations


Projects in state waters require authorization from the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers(ACOE) regardless of whether the project is grid-connected or not, unless the project activities fall within the scope of Nationwide Permit 52 (Water-Based Renewable Energy Generation Pilot Projects, Effective Date: March 19, 2017; Expiration Date: March 18, 2022). Authorization from the ACOE encompasses the following regulatory elements:
